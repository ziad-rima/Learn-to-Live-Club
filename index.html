About Us

- Learn to Live Club (LTL) is a scientific and cultural club established on October 31, 2021, at the Faculty of Hydrocarbons at Kasdi Merbah University in Ouargla.

- Founded by a team of Hydrocarbons students, the club's primary goal is to share knowledge and experiences with fellow students.

- LTL focuses on providing courses and practical training to equip students and participants with valuable skills in their field.

- While primarily focused on Petroleum Engineering majors—such as Oil & Gas Production, Drilling Engineering, Well Machining, and Mechanical Engineering, the club also covers other disciplines essential to the field.

- LTL’s mission is to foster a hardworking, well-educated, dependable, technically proficient, and multi-skilled generation that can contribute positively to the oil and gas industry in Algeria and beyond.
